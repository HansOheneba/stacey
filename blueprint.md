FAQs
• What is the booking process?
Fill out the form on this page with your details. Our team will respond usually within 2 working days to confirm availability and discuss next steps.

• How far in advance should we book?
At least 3–5 weeks in advance for local engagements. For large conferences or annual programs, earlier is always better.

• Does Stacey travel for events?
Yes. Stacey travels for events and engagements.

• Is coaching available, and how is that different from a speaking engagement?
Yes. Beyond the stage, Stacey works with individuals through one-on-one and group coaching. Coaching is a structured, ongoing process — not a one-time talk. If you are interested in personal coaching, use the contact form to indicate that and you will receive details on current availability and packages.

• What does Stacey need from event organisers to prepare?
A clear brief on your audience, the outcome you want from the session, any theme or programme context, and logistical details such as venue setup and AV availability. Stacey prepares specifically for every engagement — the more context you provide, the sharper the delivery.

• What is the fee for speaking engagements?
Fees vary depending on the type of engagement, audience size, duration, and whether travel is involved. Some community, school, and non-profit engagements are considered on a subsidised or pro bono basis. Reach out through the form and a conversation about fit and fee will follow.
